FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does Sofia actually answer my calls?
You forward your existing business phone number to Sofia's assigned line — a process that takes about two minutes. When a customer calls your number, Sofia picks up immediately, introduces herself, and handles the call based on how you've configured her. Your customers hear a professional, natural-sounding voice. Most never realize they're talking to an AI.
What happens to the information Sofia collects?
After every call, Sofia sends you an instant notification with a summary — the caller's name, phone number, what they needed, and any action taken (appointment booked, callback requested, etc.). You can also review full call transcripts at any time through your dashboard.
What if Sofia can't answer a question?
Sofia is configured specifically for your business — your services, your hours, your pricing policies. For questions she can't handle, she's trained to take a message and let the caller know you'll follow up. She never makes up answers or leaves a caller without a next step.
What's included in the free website?
Every plan includes a professionally built static website — fast, mobile-responsive, and optimized for search engines. It includes your services, pricing information, a lead capture form, and a booking link. There are no WordPress plugins, no slow page loads, and no monthly maintenance headaches. The site is yours as long as you're a client.
Is there a long-term contract?
Plans are billed on a 12-month service agreement, which is what makes the free website model possible. We're investing in your business from day one — the annual commitment protects both of us. After your first year, plans renew month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel.
How quickly can I get started?
Most clients are live within 48 hours of signing. Fill out our onboarding form, we configure Sofia for your business, and send you simple call forwarding instructions. You forward your number — takes two minutes — and Sofia starts answering immediately.
